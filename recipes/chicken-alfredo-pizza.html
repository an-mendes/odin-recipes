Chicken Alfredo pizza

Description

Chicken Alfredo pizza is a delicious alternative to traditional pizza. It's a great way to use up leftover chicken and Alfredo sauce, and it's easy to customize with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Alfredo sauce
  • Chicken
  • Spinach
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450Â°F.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread Alfredo sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Top with chicken, spinach, and cheeses.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  6. Let cool slightly before slicing and serving.
